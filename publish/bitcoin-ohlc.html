Bitcoin OHLC Chart Study for Beginners

Bitcoin crypto market ka most watched asset hai. BTC OHLC candles se learner daily, weekly aur monthly trend, volatility, breakout, pullback aur major support/resistance zones ko study kar sakta hai. Market History Lab ka goal beginner ko chart structure samjhana hai, trading signal dena nahi.

Bitcoin chart me kya important hai?

Trend direction

Higher highs/higher lows bullish structure dikha sakte hain, lower highs/lower lows bearish structure ka clue de sakte hain.

Candle range

BTC me volatility high ho sakti hai, isliye candle range aur wick size ko risk ke saath study karna chahiye.

Market structure

BOS aur CHoCH concepts trend continuation ya possible reversal samajhne me help karte hain.

Beginner checklist

  • Daily aur weekly close ko compare karo.
  • Big green/red candles ke baad pullback zones observe karo.
  • RSI, EMA aur volume ko context ke saath study karo.
  • Crypto trading high risk hoti hai; live data aur risk management verify karo.
