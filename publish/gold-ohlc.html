Gold OHLC Chart Study for Beginners

Gold, ya XAUUSD, global traders ke liye important market hai. Gold OHLC candles daily, weekly aur monthly timeframe me trend, volatility aur major reaction zones samajhne me help karti hain. Beginner learners support/resistance, liquidity sweep aur candle rejection ko Gold chart par practice kar sakte hain.

Gold chart me useful observations

Volatility

Gold me kabhi-kabhi fast moves aate hain, isliye candle wick aur range ko carefully study karna chahiye.

Support/Resistance

Jahan Gold repeatedly react kare, wo area future me important level ban sakta hai.

Monthly trend

Monthly OHLC chart long-term direction aur major zones samajhne ke liye useful hota hai.

Beginner checklist

  • Daily high/low aur weekly high/low ko mark karo.
  • Long wicks ko rejection ya liquidity sweep ke context me study karo.
  • FVG aur order block zones ko trend direction ke saath compare karo.
  • Live trading se pehle trusted source se current Gold price verify karo.
