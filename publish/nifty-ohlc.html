Nifty OHLC Chart Study for Beginners

Nifty 50 India ke most watched equity index me se ek hai. OHLC candles se learner samajh sakta hai ki market daily, weekly aur monthly timeframe me kaise move kar raha hai. Market History Lab par Nifty chart ko candle patterns, support/resistance, indicators aur market structure ke saath practice kiya ja sakta hai.

Nifty OHLC me kya dekhna chahiye?

Open and Close

Open aur close se candle ka body banta hai. Strong body trend momentum ka clue de sakti hai.

High and Low

High/low se pata chalta hai ki buyers aur sellers ne candle period me kaha tak price push kiya.

Weekly change

Weekly candle short-term noise ko kam karke broader index direction samajhne me help karti hai.

Beginner checklist

  • Daily candle se recent price action dekho.
  • Weekly candle se major trend aur support/resistance levels mark karo.
  • Doji, engulfing, inside bar aur outside bar ko context ke saath study karo.
  • RSI, EMA aur volume ko confirmation tool ki tarah use karo, blind signal ki tarah nahi.
