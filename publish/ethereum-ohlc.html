Ethereum OHLC Chart Study for Beginners

Ethereum, ya ETH, crypto market ka major asset hai. ETH OHLC candles beginner learners ko trend, volatility, support/resistance aur candle pattern behavior samajhne me help karti hain. Daily, weekly aur monthly views ko compare karke market ka broader structure clearer ho sakta hai.

Ethereum chart me useful studies

Daily candles

Daily candles recent momentum aur short-term reaction zones ko study karne ke liye useful hoti hain.

Weekly candles

Weekly view se ETH ka broader trend aur important swing high/low better samajh aata hai.

Pattern context

Doji, engulfing ya outside bar tabhi useful hota hai jab support/resistance aur trend context ke saath dekha jaye.

Beginner checklist

  • ETH ka daily close aur weekly close compare karo.
  • Important highs/lows ke around liquidity sweep observe karo.
  • EMA, RSI aur volume ko confirmation tool ki tarah rakho.
  • Real trading se pehle current exchange data verify karo.
