OHLC Quick Meaning

Open: candle kaha start hui. High: period ka top. Low: period ka bottom. Close: candle kaha end hui.

Body momentum batata hai, wick rejection batata hai. Context ke bina candle pattern use mat karo.

Doji Candle

Open aur close almost same. Hindi: market decision nahi le pa raha. English: indecision candle.

Outside Bar

Current candle previous candle ka high aur low dono todti hai. Range expansion ka signal ho sakta hai.

FVG Gap

Fast move me imbalance zone banta hai. Hindi: price kabhi kabhi is gap ko retest karta hai.

Order Block (OB)

Strong move se pehle last opposite candle ka zone. Buyers/sellers ka institutional area maan kar watchlist me mark karte hain.

Liquidity Sweep

Price previous high/low ke upar-niche jaake stop-loss collect karta hai, phir reverse kar sakta hai. Confirmation candle zaroor dekho.

BOS

Break of Structure: trend ka important high/low break hota hai. English: market structure continuation clue.

CHoCH

Change of Character: existing trend weak padta hai aur opposite structure break hota hai. Reversal warning ho sakti hai.

Support / Resistance

Jahan price baar-baar react karta hai. Single line nahi, ek zone socho. Break + retest beginner ke liye clean method hai.
