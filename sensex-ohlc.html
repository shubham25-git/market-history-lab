Sensex OHLC Chart Study for Beginners

Sensex India ke oldest aur most followed equity benchmarks me se ek hai. OHLC candles se learner samajh sakta hai ki large-cap market daily, weekly aur monthly timeframe me kaise behave kar raha hai. Market History Lab me Sensex movement ko support/resistance, candle patterns aur market structure ke context ke saath study kiya ja sakta hai.

Sensex OHLC me kya dekhna chahiye?

Daily Direction

Daily open-close relation se intraday sentiment aur trend pressure ka idea milta hai.

Weekly Structure

Weekly high/low important support aur resistance zones mark karne me help karta hai.

Monthly Trend

Monthly candle long-term market direction aur major range breakout samajhne ke liye useful hai.

Beginner checklist

  • Sensex daily candle ko Nifty ke saath compare karo.
  • Previous week high/low aur monthly open ko important level ki tarah mark karo.
  • Doji, engulfing, outside bar aur gap candles ko support/resistance ke paas study karo.
  • Price action ke saath RSI, EMA aur volume ko confirmation tool ki tarah use karo.
